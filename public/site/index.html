Sound Familiar?

Running rentals shouldn't hijack your life.

Whether you inherited a property, bought one as a side hustle, or grew a small portfolio on your own: you already have a full-time life. Managing tenants shouldn't consume it too.

📱

"My heat isn't working" — at 11pm on a Friday

Tenant emergencies don't follow business hours, and without a buffer, every notification lands directly in your personal life.

🗂️

Lease dates, receipts, and maintenance all over the place

Spreadsheets, email threads, text messages, paper files. Critical info is scattered and impossible to find when you need it fast.

💸

A property manager costs 8–12% of your rent roll

That's thousands of dollars a year leaving your pocket, just to have someone else deal with the calls you're already fielding yourself.

🔄

The same questions. Over and over again.

"Where do I pay rent?" "What's the WiFi password?" "When is trash day?" You've answered these a hundred times and you'll answer them a hundred more.

🏗️

Maintenance requests slip through the cracks

A small fix ignored becomes a big repair. Without a proper system, it's easy for requests to get lost in the noise — until a tenant is furious.

😮‍💨

You can't scale what you can't manage

You want to grow your portfolio but the thought of adding another property to your current chaos? That keeps you up at night.

"

We built TheHouseHub because we were living this problem. Late-night texts from tenants, maintenance requests buried in our inbox, lease dates we nearly missed — all while working full-time jobs. We couldn't find a tool built for landlords like us, so we built one ourselves. Everything on this page is a pain point we've personally felt.

The Solution

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built by self-managing landlords, for self-managing landlords. No bloated software designed for big property companies. No per-unit fees. No learning curve.

🤖

AI-Powered Tenant Messaging

Our built-in AI handles routine tenant messages automatically, answering FAQs, acknowledging requests, and routing real issues to you. Most tenants never even know it's not you.

🔧

Maintenance Request Tracking

Tenants submit requests through a clean portal. You get organized tickets with photos, priority levels, and status updates. Nothing falls through the cracks again.

📋

Lease & Document Management

Store leases, renewal dates, move-in checklists, and tenant documents in one searchable place. Get automated reminders before key dates so you're never caught off-guard.

💳

Rent Collection & Payment Tracking

Tenants pay online, you get paid on time. Automated late payment reminders go out without you lifting a finger, keeping things professional without awkward conversations.

🔕

Do Not Disturb Mode

Set hours when tenant notifications are silenced. The Hub still captures and triages everything. You see a clean summary when you're ready. Your evenings are yours again.

📊

Property Dashboard at a Glance

One clean dashboard shows rent status, open requests, upcoming lease dates, and maintenance logs across all your properties. Know everything in 30 seconds.

Getting Started

Up and running in under an hour.

No IT department required. No month-long onboarding. Just set it up and start getting your time back.

1

Add Your Properties

Enter your units, upload existing leases, and add tenant contact info. Takes about 3 minutes per property.

2

Invite Your Tenants

Tenants get a simple link to their portal. No app download required. They can submit requests, pay rent, and message you all in one place.

3

Let the Hub Handle It

The AI manages routine communication, requests get organized, and you only get pinged when something genuinely needs your attention.
